Ingredients
  • 250g butter, soft
  • 225g Sugar
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 4 fresh eggs
  • 1 lemon
  • 500g flour
  • Decoration of your linking
Preperation
1.
Mix the Butter in an Bowl.
After that, add Sugar and Salt to the mixure.

2.
Mix the Butter in an Bowl.
After that, add Sugar and Salt to the mixure. Crack open 3 of the 4 Eggs and add them to the mixing Bowl.
